Our Mission

We are a dedicated breeder of Ragdolls and Cherubim cats, based in Derby, UK. Established in 2016, our breeding program adheres to the high standards recognised by TICA and GCCF. Each cat/kitten comes with a Registration and Pedigree with either Tica or Gccf. The health and well-being of our cats is our top priority. All breeding parents are thoroughly tested and are clear of HCM (Ragdoll & Maine Coon) and PKD. If you’re looking for a beautiful, well-bred Ragdoll or Cherubim kitten, lovingly raised and healthy, I would be delighted to help you find your perfect companion!

  • Microchipping
