Our Mission

We are a dedicated breeder of Ragdolls and Cherubim cats, based in Derby, UK. Established in 2016, My journey began with My first Ragdolls in 2018. My breeding program adheres to the breed standards recognised by TICA and GCCF, come with: Registration and Pedigree with either Tica or Gccf • , • Full vaccinations, • Microchipping. and include toys and food and an abundance of knowledge and advice. The health and well-being of our cats is our top priority. All breeding parents are thoroughly tested and are clear of HCM (Ragdoll & Maine Coon) and PKD. If you’re looking for a beautiful, well-bred Ragdoll or Cherubim kitten, lovingly raised and healthy, I would be delighted to help you find your perfect companion!
